About Me

Hello, my name is Chris - I am a current Computer Information Systems Student at Buffalo State College. This past fall 2019 is my first semester back in school, I had previously graduated from SUNY Brockport with a B.S. degree with a double major in History and Political Science. The past few years I have worked in the insurance industry holding positions as a Commercial Insurance Account Manager, Commercial Underwriter and Claims Adjuster. I have always had an interest in all things tech and after taking some lessons online and an Intro to CS Java course at Monroe Community College I have decided to go all-in to pursue a second degree in Computer Information Systems. I am graduating this May 2020 and am actively looking for software development roles if you have any leads!

Of the many things I like to do in my free time my top hobbies are taking my dog Pax on walks, photography (I took the photo in the header, see my photography section for more!), playing soccer and watching a good TV series.
